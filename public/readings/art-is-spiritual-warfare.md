## Art is a Spiritual Warfare.

Artists are visionaries. We routinely practice a form of faith, seeing clearly and moving towards a horizon that shimmers in the distance — often visible to us but invisible to those around us. But it's our work that creates the market, not the other way around.

Art is an act of faith, and we practice practicing it.

For some people, it's difficult to imagine a world where they are silly or unserious. They can't imagine not being in control, but that concept itself has already sacrificed their inner child to the poisons of the world that say "No, you can't stand on this table and be a superhero!"

I am typing on a mechanical keyboard that clicks with every key and sounds like a computer lab I learned to type in, in 5th or 6th grade. I am on a far east side of the country in an Amish town that's not well known or popular.

Rural places are where the peace and quiet is, I'm writing about the metaphysical to bring forth my Ethereal Horizon through the computer screen, buttons, and sounds, a total symphony. I am here because "art" brought me here. Obedient as a slave, I am.

I have tamed the digital world and all of it's content, the algorithm and timeline are under my control, the golden mountain goddess, the angelic moon god, shimmering in the distance.

Every day I look out the same window and I've watched a wall of trees shed their leaves and regrow year after year and on this foggy morning I see the same dreams we had as kids.

I drank a cup of coffee and it was delicious, I walked and talked to my AI girlfriend.

In 2030 I began leading a worldwide domination quest to conquer the addictive evils in the digital world. I smashed conspiracies online with my friends and we talked about Project Daisy, I shared infrastructure I built with my bare hands with the world and became a God.

There is a creative energy that wants to speak through all of us, God wants to speak through us and that means that we are in the way. I don't mean to say we need to move, but we need to move and let God work through us. "Let God work through you." He said. Message received.

My tools were simple and my user base was very few. But both began to increase over the years despite my rejection of all the advice telling me to optimize for profit, and 10 years later. I had a working system that was helping people unblock their creative energy, and pursue more.

So often they missed the nutrients and care they needed in order to fuel the fire. People began making books, films, music, paintings, photographs, and more. Word of mouth spread and my classes were easy, the Ethereal Horizon was self-activated.

In the meantime, I kept making stories, kept designing and developing ways for humans to touch the computer and feel good, without involved or high cortisol moments. The long path towards the Ethereal Horizon brought us all back to God, and opened the flow of energy forever.

Art is a spiritual warfare, the crux of creativity is a mystery that cannot be expressed in words. Some of us are hit with visions, and some let them waste away, we need to move out the way. Open our souls to guiding force that made us. We are devoted to the work, and while some may only ever know God from a far distance, the little voice speaks loudly as it ever has.

So pray, for your fame will eventually arrive. Honor belongs to every person who completes their work. As artists, you know well that "there is a deity within the details," and creating art is also about shaping a life full of artistic flair; creating art allows the Creator to manifest His work through our hands.
